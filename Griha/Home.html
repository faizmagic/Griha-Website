Lighter than the Lightest

At 114gm the feather light Iris Pro 30 is all set to be pegged as the lightest phone in India in its category*. It's unbelievably light and carefully crafted to perfection for that ultra-luxurious smartphone experience. So, brace yourself for some supremely advanced technology wrapped in a super light body!

Seeing is believing

With a 11.93 cm (4.7) HD IPS screen packed with the One Glass Solution Technology, the Iris Pro 30 is brilliance personified. With cutting-edge technology such as the high strength Gorilla Corning Glass, we bet, you haven't seen better!

Slimmer than the slimmest

Introducing the stunning and svelte Iris Pro 30 at just 7.5mm. This ergonomically designed device has been fused with extraordinary technology that fits perfectly in your hand. We promise you a plush blend of style and technical brilliance.

Performance that’ll leave you speechless!

Get ready to experience unmatched performance with the new IRIS Pro 30. Powered by the 1.2GHz Quad Core Processor and an advanced CABC battery, this device not just ensures smooth functioning but also lasts as long as your heart desires.

That’s just as strong as it can get!

Fashioned from the high strength Corning Gorilla Glass, the new Iris Pro 30 is your partner in the test of strength. It’s tough and less sensitive towards scratches. This ensures that your phone keeps looking new even after months of using it.

Down the memory lane

Now, walk down the fond alleys of memory whenever, wherever. Presenting the new Lava Iris Pro 30 with an 8MP back camera and 3MP front camera for those perfect clicks and selfies. Get ready to get clicking!

That’s just how convenient it is!

If you are one of those who values convenience above everything else, this just the device for you. Presenting the new Lava Iris Pro 30 that enables you to pick up and disconnect calls by just lifting the phone. It also allows you to unlock the device through a voice command and face tracking.
